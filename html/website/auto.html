Auto Insurance

Your car may be your pride and joy. It may be a simple means of transportation. Whatever your auto insurance needs, we will provide personalized service to find the right plan. We’ll make sure you get all the auto insurance benefits you are qualified to receive:

Good Driver
Take advantage of being a good driver. If you have an outstanding driving record let us know. Depending on your driving history we can help you save money on your auto insurance! If you avoid tickets and accidents you can be rewarded for being a “good driver”. Ask us how.

Good Student
If you have a young student driver that you are or will be insuring, let us know. Good grades are important in more ways than one. Reap the benefits of those hard earned grades by receiving he “good student” discount on your auto insurance. Ask us for details.

Auto Safety Features
Today’s cars are built with safety in mind. Newer cars includes air bags, anti-lock brakes, anti-theft devices and other options that can help reduce your auto insurance premiums. Ask us how your car stacks up.

Home/Auto Insurance Package
We do our best to provide full coverage to our customers wherever possible. Combining home and auto insurance into our special combo packages can reduce your overall insurance costs for the long run.

Senior Citizen
Let your years of great driving speak for itself. Great drivers deserve great rates. We can also find insurance for high-risk drivers and we offer SR-22’s.
