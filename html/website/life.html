Life Insurance

Life insurance is a necessity because it is the safety net for those you love the most – your family. It is also an excellent way of investing for the future and gaining tax benefits. We can also set up life insurance plans for your company or small business. For your diverse life insurance needs, we handle all of the following

Variable Universal Life and Whole Life
Variable Universal Life offers flexibility for premiums and death benefits and also has the potential to increase its cash value based on the performance of underlying funds that you choose. If you want to be in control, then variable universal life is for you. Because it is tied to many securities markets it may provide important protection against inflation. Ask us about he pros and cons of Variable universal life insurance for you and your family.

Term Insurance
Think of term life insurance as the strict cost of being insured. Term life insurance builds no cash value so the premiums for term life insurance is low. Term life insurance can be converted to variable universal life and whole life. Term life insurance pays a pre-determined amount of money to your beneficiary in the case of death and death benefits are tax free. Ask us how we can find premiums and a term life insurance plan to fit your budget.

Annuity, IRA, 401K/Pension Rollover
We’ll help you prepare for retirement. You will be surprised how far a little planning will go. Putting a little bit away on a consistent basis is the secret to building a retirement nest egg. We can arrange automatic deductions that will help you save for the future instead of spending now.
