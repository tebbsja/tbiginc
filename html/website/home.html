Home Insurance

What is more important than your home? Along with providing a safe haven for your family, your home is one of your key financial investments. For your peace of mind, your coverage needs to match the fluctuating value of your home.
You also need that same assurance that claims will be settled promptly and completely. We help you find the policy that is right for you. Possible discounts on home owners insurance include:

New Home Discount
New homes are built with higher standards in fire-resistant building materials. Furnaces, wiring and major appliances are built to a higher standard than ever before. Take advantage of your new home with a “new home discount”. Let us help you.

Security System Discount
Having a security system in your home is not only a smart idea but it can bring peace of mind to the whole family. A security system can also save you money on your home owners insurance. Ask us how.

Mature Owner Discount
If you’re age fifty five or older, we can reduce the cost of your home owners insurance. Statistics consistently show that mature home owners will have fewer and less costly claims, so why not pass the savings on to you.

Home/Auto Insurance Package
We do our best to provide full coverage to our customers wherever possible. Combining home and auto insurance into our special combo packages can reduce your overall insurance costs for the long run.

Non-smoker Discount
If your household is a non-smoking household we may be able to help you receive an additional discount.
We can help you with insurance for mobile or manufactured homes. We also offer coverage for earthquakes, floods, and other catastrophes and for such scheduled items as jewelry, furs, guns, fine arts, and collectibles.
